12 What is the name for the course of study in a school?
13 What is the word for people who cannot read or write?
14 What is the work of UNESCO in Ethiopia?
15 Name three countries that the Rift Valley runs through.

17 Which is the longest river in Africa?
18 What is the name for when the sea wears away the land?
